Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is Skribb AI's handwriting recognition?

Skribb AI achieves up to 80% accuracy on clear handwriting. Accuracy may vary based on handwriting quality, image resolution, and language. Our AI continuously improves through machine learning and user feedback.

What file formats are supported for upload?

We support JPG, PNG, TIFF, BMP, and WebP image formats. For best results, use high-resolution images (at least 300 DPI) with good lighting and minimal shadows.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your personal data with third parties. You can delete your data at any time. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What languages does Skribb AI support?

Currently, we support English with excellent accuracy. We're actively working on adding support for Spanish, French, German, and other major languages. Multi-language support will be available in upcoming updates.

Can I use Skribb AI for commercial purposes?

Yes, our paid plans include commercial usage rights. Free tier users can use the service for personal projects. For enterprise needs, contact us for custom pricing and features.

How do I improve OCR accuracy for my handwriting?

For best results: use dark ink on white paper, ensure good lighting, avoid shadows, write clearly with adequate spacing, and upload high-resolution images. You can also provide corrections to help our AI learn your handwriting style.
