Capture Your Notes

Multiple ways to get your handwritten content into Skribb AI. Choose the method that works best for you.

Smartphone Camera

Simply snap a photo of your notes with your phone's camera. The AI works best with good lighting conditions.

Document Scanner

Use any document scanner or scanning app to create high-quality digital images of your handwritten notes.

File Upload

Drag and drop existing images of your notes, or browse and select files from your device.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Ensure good lighting - natural light works best
  • Keep the camera parallel to the paper surface
  • Include the entire page with some margin space
  • Avoid shadows and reflections on the paper

Common questions about how Skribb AI works

The AI achieves upto 80%+ accuracy on most handwriting styles. The system is trained on millions of handwriting samples and continuously improves. For best results, ensure good lighting and clear photos. Even with messy handwriting, our context-aware AI can often understand and correct obvious errors.

Your privacy is our priority. Images are processed securely in the cloud and automatically deleted within 24 hours. We never store your personal notes permanently. All processing is encrypted, and we comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.
